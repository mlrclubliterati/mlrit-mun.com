Secretary General

It gives me immense pleasure in welcoming you to MLRIT MUN. Hosted by one of the most distinguished and prestigious colleges in the state, returns with its 3nd edition, with a promise of being a platform to debate and deliberate like never before.
    The world today stands at a critical juncture. The issues faced by it are multifold manifesting in a number of forms be it social, economic, political and even legal in many circumstances. With the interests of so many nations, religions, ethnicities, communities and multiple other subsets, reaching an unanimous course of action to resolve these issues is becoming bleaker and more challenging.
    This is a deliberation that will initiate and strengthen the passion for international relations, debate and politics in the world. The five varied committees this year aim to expose the delegates to the vast spectrum of issues faced. The committees simulated will be discussing the most pressing contemporary issues faced, and in doing so, calling upon delegates to exercise their skills of debate and diplomacy, and striking the precarious balance between the two.
    If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, do not hesitate to contact us. We will gladly assist you to resolve any issues you may encounter on your way to MLRIT MUN. We are looking forward to host you and your delegation for a 3-days session full of substantive and exciting debate in Sept 2017.
    On behalf of the MLRIT MUN Team and MLR Institute of Technology, I welcome you and your delegation to MLRIT MUN 2017! All the best!

Rahul Kintada
